Link to the Cure: New Game+

One incredible weekend has come to a close, but the adventure is far from over.

For the first time ever, Link to the Cure returns with a second event in the same year.

New Game+ is our celebration of creative speedrunning. Traditional speedruns, challenge runs, randomizers, Crowd Control, No-Hit attempts, and unique categories are all welcome.

Runner submissions officially open on July 1st. Until then, start planning, practice those routes, and prepare for another incredible weekend of gaming for a great cause.
