About Child's Play

Link to the Cure proudly supports Child's Play, a charity focused on bringing comfort and joy to children in hospitals through games and play. Since 2003, Child's Play has helped provide games, toys, books, and technology to pediatric hospitals and shelters worldwide.

All donations made during Link to the Cure go directly to Child's Play through the event's fundraising platforms.
